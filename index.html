How to Play

  • 🎲 Roll the dice to traverse the Superchain
  • 🏠 Land on SuperSeed properties to stake your claim
  • 🎮 Master mini-games to secure properties:
    • - Flappy Seeds: Score 10 SuperGM points
    • - Quiz: Prove your SuperSeed knowledge
  • 💰 Harvest SUPR rent when others land on your turf
  • 🏆 Be the last Supercollateral holder to reign supreme! (this means to win :D )

This is a game that will make you learn many things about the Superseed and also have fun with your friends!
